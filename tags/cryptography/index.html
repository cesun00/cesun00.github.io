Specification Makers in Cryptography

March 30, 2024
ITU-T and X.* ITU (International Telecommunication Union ) is a United Nations affiliated organization. ITU-T (ITU Telecommunication Standardization Sector) is one if ITU’s 3 sub-departments that works on standardization of telecommunications. ITU-T is further divided into study groups from SG2 to SG20. Those study groups release standard specifications (they call “recommendations”) identified by <letter>.<number>. For example X means Data networks, open system communications and security, and X.509 identifies a specific document in X series.
